// about

      
Building models that hold up outside the notebook

      

        

          I'm a final-year B.Sc. Artificial Intelligence & Data Science student at
          Padmavani Arts & Science College, Salem, working toward a career as a
          Data Scientist / Machine Learning Engineer. Across two internships and four
          independent projects, I've worked the full pipeline — cleaning messy datasets,
          engineering features that actually move the needle, training and tuning
          classifiers, and shipping the result behind a Streamlit or Flask interface
          so it's usable by someone who isn't reading the code.
        

        

          I like problems where the data is imperfect and the stakes are real: predicting
          accident severity from incomplete police records, or squeezing extra
          precision out of a churn model with six candidate algorithms. I work in
          Agile sprints, document as I go, and care as much about a model's
          precision/recall trade-off as I do about whether a non-technical
          stakeholder can understand what it's telling them.
